# DailyDump.js 💩
## 🌐 Live Demo

You can try out the live version of this project directly in your browser:
👉 [Launch DailyDump.js](https://chilldrenchi.github.io/DailyDump.js/)

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)

**DailyDump.js** is a minimalist, web-based dashboard designed for professionals who need to maintain strict "output" logs during office hours. You can track your cycle with a high-performance, dark-themed, zero-latency local storage solution.

---

## 🚀 Key Features
- **Office-Optimized:** Perfectly disguised as a productivity dashboard.
- **Data-Driven:** Visualizing your metabolic trends with real-time line charts.
- **Local-First:** All your data stays strictly in your browser. No backend, no cookies, no tracking.
- **Minimalist:** Optimized for those quick "breaks".

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Engine:** Vanilla JavaScript
- **Visualization:** [Chart.js](https://www.chartjs.org/) + [Chartjs-plugin-datalabels](https://chartjs-plugin-datalabels.netlify.app/)
- **Storage:** Browser LocalStorage

## 💡 How to Use
1. Open the app in your browser.
2. Click **Pee 💧** or **Poop 💩** to log your activity.
3. Observe your weekly trends in the visual chart.
4. Hit "Clear History" to reset your stats.
